Commercial Flight Stretcher

We specialize in worldwide patient transfer, providing FAA-approved equipment on commercial flights along with a professional air ambulance flight medics. Our goal is to make every medical journey safe, comfortable, and cost-effective.

What Is a Commercial Flight Stretcher Service?

A commercial flight stretcher is a specially arranged area on a commercial airline designed for patients who cannot sit upright during travel. This setup uses FAA-approved equipment installed by the airline's engineers to accommodate both the patient and the necessary medical equipment. Our air-ambulance trained medical team accompanies the patient throughout the journey, ensuring continuous monitoring, oxygen support, and compassionate care. This option provides the same level of medical safety as an air ambulance, but at a fraction of the cost, making it ideal for international medical repatriation and long-distance patient transfer.

Ensuring Patient Privacy and Comfort

At Air Medical 24X7, we place the patient's comfort and dignity first. The commercial airline stretcher area is enclosed with privacy curtains, ensuring the patient is shielded from view while traveling. Our certified flight doctors, nurses, and paramedics monitor the patient's condition in real time, ensuring a smooth and stable transfer. Family members or friends can accompany the patient on the same commercial flight, offering emotional support throughout the journey, something not always possible with private air ambulances.

Airline Stretcher Service

When patients cannot sit through a long journey due to illness or injury, our airline stretcher service provides a safe and affordable alternative to chartered air ambulance flights.

Just like an air ambulance, we supply:

  • Trained medical escorts.
  • Advanced medical equipment.
  • Continuous inflight monitoring.
  • Oxygen and emergency medications.

However, by using a commercial airline rather than a dedicated aircraft, patients enjoy equal safety and medical attention at significantly lower cost.

How Air Medical 24X7 Handles Every Detail

Arranging a commercial stretcher flight or air ambulance transfer involves expert coordination and that's where we excel.

Our bed-to-bed transfer process includes:

  • Medical Evaluation: Consultation with the treating physician to assess eligibility.
  • Documentation: Gathering medical reports and the Fit-to-Fly certificate.
  • Airline & Medical Clearance: We manage all airline approvals and stretcher bookings.
  • Ground Ambulance Transfers: Pickup and drop at both ends of the journey.
  • Medical Team Assignment: ICU-trained doctors and/or flight medics accompany every flight.
  • Flight & Companion Ticketing: Arrangements for family members to travel alongside.
  • Continuous Updates: Real-time updates to family members throughout the transfer.

Who Can Benefit from a Commercial Flight Stretcher?

Our services are ideal for patients who:

  • Are medically stable but unable to sit upright for long periods.
  • Have undergone surgery or sustained trauma.
  • Require oxygen, IV fluids, or continuous monitoring.
  • Need international air ambulance repatriation.
  • Prefer a cost-effective, safe, and comfortable alternative to private air ambulances.
