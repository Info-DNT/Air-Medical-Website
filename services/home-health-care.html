Home Health Care

Air Medical 24X7 extends professional, hospital-standard medical care into the patient's home. Recovery is often best supported in familiar surroundings, and our home health care service brings skilled nursing, monitoring, and rehabilitation support directly to the patient - from post-discharge recovery to ongoing clinical care.

For many patients, the transition from hospital to home is a vulnerable period that requires continued clinical attention. Our service bridges that gap, reducing the strain of hospital visits while maintaining a high standard of care, with every plan supervised by qualified professionals and coordinated with the patient's treating physicians.

Why Choose Home Health Care with Air Medical 24X7?

Air Medical 24X7 delivers personalized, professionally supervised care at home, with continuous clinical oversight and seamless coordination with the wider medical team.

Key Features:

  • Skilled Nursing: Professional post-discharge and recovery care at home.
  • Clinical Monitoring: Regular vital-sign assessment and condition tracking.
  • Medication Management: Accurate administration and oversight of medication.
  • Rehabilitation Support: Physiotherapy and recovery assistance.
  • Physician Coordination: Continuous communication with the patient's treating doctors.
  • Personalized Care Plans: Tailored to each patient's condition and needs.

Services Offered

1. Post-Discharge Care

Continued clinical support during the critical recovery period after hospital discharge.

2. Skilled Nursing Visits

Professional nursing care delivered at home, as required.

3. Rehabilitation and Physiotherapy

Structured recovery support to restore mobility and strength.

4. Ongoing Clinical Support

Long-term care management for patients with continuing needs.

Our Process

Following a clinical assessment, we develop a personalized care plan aligned with the patient's medical condition and recovery goals. Qualified healthcare professionals then deliver care at home according to that plan, with regular review and coordination to keep the patient's wider medical team fully informed.

Questions That Are Often Asked

1. What does home health care include?

It includes skilled nursing, clinical monitoring, medication management, rehabilitation support, and coordination with the patient's treating physicians — all delivered at home.

2. Who provides the care at home?

Care is delivered by qualified, experienced healthcare professionals under continuous clinical oversight.

3. Is the care plan tailored to each patient?

Yes. Every care plan is personalized to the patient's specific condition, recovery goals, and needs.
