ECMO Transfer Services

Air Medical 24X7 is a trusted and leading provider of ECMO transfer services worldwide. We offer safe, reliable, and fully coordinated transport for critically ill patients whose heart or lungs require advanced life support during transfer. Every ECMO mission is managed by a specialist clinical team and conducted aboard aircraft configured as fully equipped intensive care units in the sky.

ECMO transport is among the most complex procedures in aeromedical care, demanding precise planning, specialized equipment, and continuous expert supervision. From the initial medical assessment through to bed-to-bed handover, our team ensures uninterrupted cardiac and respiratory support across every stage of the journey — domestically and internationally.

Why Choose ECMO Transfer Services with Air Medical 24X7?

Air Medical 24X7 specializes in high-acuity critical care transport, supported by an experienced clinical team and a worldwide aircraft network capable of responding wherever and whenever required.

Key Features:

  • Specialist ECMO Team: Trained intensivists, perfusionists, and critical care specialists on board every transfer.
  • Flying ICU Configuration: Aircraft equipped as fully functional intensive care units for uninterrupted life support.
  • FAA-Approved Equipment: All onboard medical devices meet the highest international safety and quality standards.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Real-time cardiac and respiratory monitoring throughout the flight.
  • Bed-to-Bed Service: Seamless transfer from one medical facility to another, without interruption in care.
  • 24/7 Availability: Our coordination team operates around the clock, 365 days a year.
  • Transparent Pricing: Clear, all-inclusive cost estimates with no hidden charges.

Our Process

Every ECMO transfer begins with a detailed medical evaluation, typically completed well in advance of departure, to confirm the patient's clinical stability and transport readiness. Our team then coordinates aircraft configuration, clinical staffing, and synchronized ground ambulances at both origin and destination — ensuring the patient transitions seamlessly between facilities under continuous specialist supervision.

Equipment and Safety Standards

We maintain the highest safety standards with FAA-approved medical equipment on board, including:

  • ECMO Life-Support Systems: For continuous heart and lung support during transit.
  • Advanced Ventilators: For respiratory support.
  • Cardiac Monitors: To track vital signs throughout the transfer.
  • Defibrillators: For cardiac emergencies.
  • Infusion Pumps and Oxygen Supply: For controlled medication and breathing support.
  • Temperature-Controlled Environment: For patient comfort and stability.

Questions That Are Often Asked

1. What is ECMO and when is it required?

ECMO is an advanced life-support technique that temporarily takes over the function of the heart and lungs. It is used for patients with severe cardiac or respiratory failure who cannot maintain adequate oxygenation on their own.

2. Can a patient on ECMO be transported safely by air?

Yes. With a specialist ECMO team, the correct equipment, and careful planning, ECMO patients can be transported safely. Our aircraft are configured as flying ICUs and staffed by clinicians trained specifically in extracorporeal life support.

3. How is the cost of an ECMO transfer determined?

Costs depend on distance, aircraft type, the medical team required, and the patient's specific clinical needs. We provide a clear, all-inclusive quotation in advance.
