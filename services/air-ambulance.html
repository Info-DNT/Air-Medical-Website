Air Ambulance Services

At Air Medical 24X7, we provide urgent air ambulance services offering fast and efficient critical care transfer and neonatal evacuation services around the clock. Whether it's a medical emergency, accident, or need for specialized transfer, we ensure the fastest and safest journey for you.

Our Services

Air Medical 24X7 offers a range of air ambulance services, each designed to meet specific patient needs. Our fleet is fully equipped and staffed by highly trained medical professionals, ensuring safe and efficient transfer in every scenario.

Critical Care Transfer

We provide critical care air ambulance services for patients requiring immediate medical transfer. Whether it's trauma, cardiac arrest, stroke, or other life-threatening conditions, our air ambulances are equipped with Advanced Life Support (ALS) systems and expert teams ensuring emergency care during transfer.

Neonatal Air Ambulance Services

Our neonatal services are specially designed for premature or critically ill newborns. We deploy neonatal nurses and respiratory specialists capable of managing complex cases such as congenital defects and respiratory challenges during transit.

International Air Ambulance Transfer

We arrange immediate global medical transfer regardless of location. With experienced staff and international assistance partners, we ensure safe transfer using advanced equipment onboard.

Bed-to-Bed Service

Our bed-to-bed medical transfer ensures continuous care. We coordinate the following:

  • Fit-to-fly medical evaluation with hospital doctors
  • Receiving hospital bed arrangements
  • Ambulance-to-aircraft shifting support
  • Medical escort assistance
