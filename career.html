Flight Doctor

Full-Time | Dubai/Delhi

Operations Coordinator

Requirements: Experience in flight logistics, aviation ground handling, or emergency coordination.

Full-Time | Dubai/Delhi

Medical Escort

Requirements: Registered Nurse or Paramedic with commercial airline experience. BLS/ACLS certified. Ability to accompany patients on international commercial flights.

Full-Time | Dubai/Delhi

Aviation Operations Manager

Requirements: 5+ years in international charter flight operations or aviation logistics. Experience coordinating with CAA/DGCA authorities and handling flight permits across multiple countries.

Full-Time | Dubai/Delhi

Patient Relationship Coordinator

Requirements: Background in healthcare coordination or patient services. Strong communication skills, multilingual preferred. Experience handling sensitive medical cases and family liaison.

Full-Time | Dubai/Delhi

Quality & Compliance Officer

Requirements: Knowledge of ISO 9001:2015, FAA/DGCA regulatory standards, and healthcare quality management. Experience in auditing and compliance within aviation or medical services.

Full-Time | Dubai/Delhi

Medical Tourism Executive
